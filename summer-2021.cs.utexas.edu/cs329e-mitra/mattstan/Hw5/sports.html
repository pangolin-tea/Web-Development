Baseball Star Babe Ruth Comes Back From Dead

Baseball legend Babe Ruth was resurrected from the dead on Monday after the MLB decided they needed more publicity to increase ticket sales. The act is being called by many an 'act against God' and 'cutting the line between life and death with a baseball bat'.

Football Declared to be Renamed to Handball

Football was recently declared to be officially renamed to Handball after a debate within the NBA over the confusion of the sport in any country other than the US.

Swimmer Micheal Phelps has decided to swim the Atlantic Ocean in search of Eldritch God

Olympic gold medalist, Micheal Phelps, has decided to swim the Atlantic Ocean in search of a long lost diety who was supposedly known to be intended to rule the Earth, but was snatched of the opportunity after a devious relative to the deity tricked the diety into an eternal slumber.
